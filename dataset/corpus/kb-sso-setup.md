# Setting up single sign-on (SSO)

SSO is available on the Business plan. Northwind Cloud supports SAML 2.0 with identity providers such as Okta and Microsoft Entra ID. To enable it, go to Settings > Security > SSO, upload your IdP metadata XML, and map the email attribute. Once enabled, members sign in through your IdP and local passwords are disabled for the organisation.
