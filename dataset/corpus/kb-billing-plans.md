# Plans and pricing

Northwind Cloud offers three plans: Free, Team, and Business. The Free plan is limited to three members and one project. The Team plan adds unlimited projects and priority email support. The Business plan adds SSO, audit logs, and a dedicated success manager. Plans are billed monthly or annually; annual billing saves two months.
